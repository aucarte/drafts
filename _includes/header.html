Square Animals Gallery
Square Animals are a collection of animals drawn using only rectangles and plain colors. The aim is to show animals from a different perspective and how versatile they are, as they have characteristics that every human being can recognize, so when you see a cat, for example, you know it's a cat because of its peculiar characteristics , similarly occurs when you see a dog, or a snake, or an elephant, as they all have specific and general characteristics that make them easy to recognize. This collection seeks to show exactly how these peculiarities of each animal can be represented in a simple, elegant and beautiful way.
