Square Animals Gallery
"Square Animals" is a collection of animal designs using just rectangles and colors. The aim is to show animals from a different perspective and how they are peculiar, as they have specific characteristics, but at the same time they are familiar to almost all human beings. For example, when you see a cat, you know it's a cat because of its peculiar characteristics, it occurs in a similar way when you see a dog, or a snake, or an elephant, because all animals have specific characteristics, but at the same time they are familiar to us, meaning we can recognize animals basically because of their peculiarities. So, this collection has the purpose of showing exactly the peculiarities of some animals and for that I tried to represent them in a simple, elegant and peculiar way. Hope it's nice!
